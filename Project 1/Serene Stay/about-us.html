About Us

Serene Stay was founded in 2011 by the Beyond Travels Association which seeks to promote a healthy and enjoyable lifestyle through their various ventures in spas, yoga clubs and gyms.

Serene Stay has always sought to be a safe haven for those who are overwhelmed, stressed and need an escape from the outside world. This is why we have a strict policy of no technological devices.

We seek to help guests deepen their connection with nature and themselves through staying with us. We provide a transcendent experience that rejuvenates and re-energizes our guests.
